Experience

Ergonomist

The Workers Compensation Board of Alberta 2025 - Present

I conduct ergonomic assessments and perform both physical and cognitive demands analyses to support safe and sustainable work practices. I also deliver engaging office and industrial workshops, equipping companies with practical strategies to reduce risk. My focus is on preventing injuries and improving worker well-being across Alberta.

Ergonomist Intern

EWI Works May 2025 - July 2025

I helped organizations improve worker health and safety through ergonomic assessments in office, vehicle, and industrial environments. I also supported research into innovative ergonomic tools, bringing forward practical solutions that reduce injuries and enhance workplace performance.

Internal Ergonomist

The Workers Compensation Board of Alberta 2022-2025

I provided ergonomic assessments and recommendations to support injured workers in safely returning to their jobs. I scored above 94% on injury prevention audits in consecutive years. I collaborated with employers, healthcare providers, and case managers to identify workplace risks, implement practical solutions, and promote long-term recovery and injury prevention.

Ergonomic Consultant

Contractor 2025 - Present

I conducted ergonomic assessments for employers to prevent injuries and delivered training/workshop sessions to employees to promote awareness.

Ergonomics Student

Advanced Ergonomics Studies - Fanshaw College 2024 - 2025

I specialized in ergonomics, human factors, and health and safety, completing hands-on projects such as evaluating a passive exoskeleton for reducing back strain, designing safer roadway signage, creating user friendly movie streaming app designs and evaluating compliance of work to regulations. These experiences strengthened my ability to apply research and design principles to improve workplace safety and performance.

Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology

University of Alberta 2018 - 2022

I earned my Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology, where I gained a strong foundation in human movement, injury prevention, and rehabilitation. I worked with collegiate athletes, kids, and the general population to support recovery and performance, building skills that I now apply to workplace health and ergonomics.
